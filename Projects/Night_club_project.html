The "Corridor" Nightclub is a unique 8420 м² structure located on the banks of the Moscow River. The club is partially embedded in the sandy landscape, creating an architectural landmark unlike anything else in the capital and becoming a new hotspot in the city's vibrant life.

The nightclub's structure is made of solid concrete with the use of wooden and metal elements. Most of the building is hidden underground, offering several advantages, including natural soundproofing, enhanced thermal insulation, and an atmosphere of seclusion.

The system of corridors connecting the dance floors allows for a smooth flow throughout the club, revealing new areas for relaxation and socializing. Along the corridors, there are special niches—buffer zones—where guests can take a break between dance sets.

Each dance floor in the club is a unique world, offering its own distinct atmosphere and spatial composition. The club features a total of four dance floors, allowing multiple performances to take place simultaneously, giving guests a variety of musical genres and moods to experience.

During the day, the club transforms under the influence of natural light streaming through skylight “wells,” radically altering the perception of the space.

In daylight hours, the club functions as an exhibition space, where guests can enjoy displays or spend time in the restaurant. The dance floors, which come alive at night to the rhythm of music, transform into exhibition halls by day, providing a setting for themed displays and offering visitors a unique, immersive atmosphere to experience art.

The club's façade seamlessly blends with the surrounding environment, turning the area around and above it into a cozy park space. Sand and nature gently embrace the architecture, creating a sense of harmony, while guests, peeking into the deep skylight wells, can catch glimpses of the mysterious inner life of the "Corridor" nightclub.
