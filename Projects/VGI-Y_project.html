VGI-Y is a design project for a 72 m² apartment executed in a minimalistic style.

A deft combination of concrete and wood is complemented by carefully selected lighting, which includes fixtures that mimic the natural daylight cycle. Such an approach to working with materials and details has allowed the creation of a unique interior where every element serves both a functional and aesthetic purpose.

This design is a prime example of how minimalism can be not only functional but also cozy.
