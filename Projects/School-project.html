The school blocks are divided into several functional volumes, visually distinguished by different materials and textures. The main two-story building, clad in milky brick, creates a sense of monumentality and reliability. Its massive structure seems to float above the first floor, which is finished with perforated metal panels that give the façade lightness and transparency.

The building is intersected by glazed passages that connect different parts of the complex and provide natural lighting to the interior spaces. The façade of the leisure center stands out with cladding made of louvers, giving it strict visual stability and orderliness, as well as emphasizing its functional independence. The composition is complemented by open spaces and inner courtyards, creating a dynamic environment for communication and relaxation. The architectural solution unites a variety of functions and materials into a single harmonious structure, forming an inspiring space for learning and development.

Project team: A. Staborovsky, J. Ardabyevskaya, A. Ovchinnikova, V. Gubanov.
