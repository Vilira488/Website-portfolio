About me

My name is Valeriy, I am an architect and designer whose work reflects a pursuit of purity and simplicity. In every project be it architecture, interior design, or furniture design I embody my philosophy.

Work

In a world where the endless stream of distracting details blurs our focus and erases the boundaries of our identity, it is crucial to create spaces where one can rediscover oneself. I strive to design environments where time slows down, allowing individuals to step out of the chaos and immerse themselves in silence. In these spaces, every detail serves as a reminder that true harmony is born from within.
